JSDoc

Extracts text from legacy Microsoft Word 97–2003 binary .doc files — entirely in your browser. Pure JavaScript, zero dependencies, clean-room from the [MS-DOC] / [MS-CFB] specs, 0BSD.

Drop a .doc here, or click to browse
Nothing is uploaded — extraction runs locally on this page.
100% client-side · no network 


    

    

      
      
      
    

  


  

    Source & tests:
    github.com/Alpaq92/JSDoc.
    Lossy by design (main-body text + paragraph breaks); returns nothing for Word 6/95,
    encrypted, or non-.doc files, exactly like the host's download fallback.
    The “Formatted” view rebuilds tables from the extracted tab/newline structure — it is
    not a pixel-faithful render of the original document.
